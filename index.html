The Journey of Aunt Betty

Snapshots of an online travel agent and world renowned adventurer

Your Aunt Betty always dreamed of adventures

Growing up in a small town creates a yearning to see the world, and Aunt Betty had it bad. Even from a young age, you’d more likely see her with her nose in an atlas than a picture book.

Although Aunt Betty graduated top of her class in archaeology and ancient history, she turned down job offers from universities and museums to embark on a solo adventure around the globe, travelling everywhere from Arizona to Barcelona and Peru to Kathmandu.
